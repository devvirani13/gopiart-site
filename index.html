What it does

One system, the whole front-of-clinic.

cscare replaces the patchwork of phone diaries, WhatsApp forwards, and paper prescriptions with a single quiet layer running behind your clinic.

01

AI phone reception

Every call is answered, in the language the patient speaks, and booked straight into your calendar — no missed calls, no double-booking.

02

Clinical notes, structured

The doctor speaks naturally during the visit; cscare turns it into a clean, structured note — ready for the record, not a typing chore.

03

Patient follow-through
